One-Pan Lemon Herb Pasta

Found on "The Simple Kitchen"

format_list_numbered Instructions

1

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook linguine according to package directions.

2

Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté garlic until fragrant.

3

Stir in lemon zest, juice, and fresh herbs. Season with salt and cracked pepper to taste.

Time 25 mins
Servings 4 people

shopping_basket Ingredients

8 items
  • Linguine pasta 400g
  • Extra virgin olive oil 1/4 cup
  • Garlic, minced 3 cloves
  • Lemon zest & juice 1 large
  • Fresh parsley 1/2 cup
