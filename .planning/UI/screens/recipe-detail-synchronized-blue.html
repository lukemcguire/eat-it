Smoked Salmon with Dill

A traditional Nordic breakfast or light lunch, highlighting fresh ingredients and clean flavors.

timer

Prep Time

15 mins

local_fire_department

Calories

350 kcal

group

Servings

2 ppl

Ingredients

Smoked Salmon 200g
Baby Potatoes 300g
Fresh Dill 1 bunch
Capers 1 tbsp
Red Onion 1/2 small
Crème Fraîche 100ml
Lemon Juice 1 tbsp

Instructions

1

Prepare the Potatoes

Boil the baby potatoes in salted water until tender, about 10-12 minutes. Drain and allow to cool slightly before slicing in halves.

2

Dress the Vegetables

In a bowl, toss the warm potatoes with lemon juice, a drizzle of olive oil, and half of the chopped dill. Season with sea salt and cracked pepper.

3

Arrange the Salmon

Lay the smoked salmon slices elegantly across two plates. Drape them loosely to create volume and texture on the plate.

4

Final Assembly

Add the potato halves, finely sliced red onions, and capers. Garnish with remaining fresh dill and a dollop of crème fraîche on the side.
